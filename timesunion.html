Times Union Case Study

For my User Interface Design II class, the goal was to find a news website that could be redesigned. This project was created using Flinto, meaning that end product could contain a variety of interactions. This project involved designing for multiple different breakpoints at the mobile, tablet, and desktop. Each breakpoint in this project has 5 unique interactions.

Issues

One of the first issues myself and those interviewed found in the site was clutter. The home page is separated into blocks which offset. The site presents a too much information to the user at once, making it hard to tell what to focus on and what isnâ€™t.

Wireframes

When wireframing, my goal was to show the most important content, while avoiding and removing the clutter that was evident with the sites current design. I placed the main story all the way on the top of the page, and put the most popular stories from each news category below. I also made sure that the most popular and latest stories were on the page.
